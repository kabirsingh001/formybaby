To the Love of My Life

My dearest Tanisha,
ever since I've known you your personality has had the most extraordinary effect on me. I grow jealous of anyone you speak to. I want to have you all to myself. I am only happy when I am with you. When you are away from me, you are still present in my heart. I can explain this feeling; words are not enough. So you understand this feeling, I hardly understood it myself. I only know and I knew then that I have seen perfection face to face and that the world had become wonderful in my eyes. You are for me the visible incarnation of everything I have ever wished for, meri jaan.
